The Engineering Career Expo second edition (ECX 2.0) has journeyed the past one year actively in the course of imparting soft and technical skills needed for a 21st century engineer to students.The journey has been quite a rollercoaster, from numerous classes to webinars and coding/design competitions. You reading this have stuck with us through the ride.There couldn’t be a better way for us to show appreciation than to showcase how much ECX has been able to achieve with your continuous support. Sit back and sip a soft drink as we take back in time to the very beginning of the journey.

Timeline

As we all know, pictures can only explain so much , so we have a detailed summary of what went down all year round.

JANUARY-ABOUT ECX

We started the peculiar year 2020 with the ECX info session to create awareness to the students especially the freshers about ECX, what we do, our core values, mission and how to join the community.


Read
FEBRUARY-CALL TO REGISTER

The ECX application form was officially opened for interested students to register for our weekly classes and get to be members of our community.


Read
MARCH-CLASSES BEGIN!

In this month we introduced and began weekly classes, held on Wednesdays and Fridays for students in the following tracks: Android Design, Back End Development, Engineering Design, Front End Development, Python and UI/UX Design.

Front-End Web Development, University of Lagos
