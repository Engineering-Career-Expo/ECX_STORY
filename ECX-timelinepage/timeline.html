MAY - MAY CHALLENGE + THE NEXT CONFERENCE

The ravaging pandemic that hit our space gave room for much to be done. In this month we had two events which were the May Challenge and The NEXT Conference MAY CHALLENGE What’s ECX without some fun? We understand how uncertain the time was with the CoronaVirus pandemic and everything going on around the world. We wanted to lighten the mood and atmosphere so we introduced the May challenge.This was a fun game where students answered light-hearted questions about their ECX experiences - their favorite parts about ECX, its tutors and a lot more! THE NEXT CONFERENCE The ECX community saw the need to enlighten its students on certain issues during the pandemic in accordance to its mission, empower young and vibrant students in not just technical, but also life-demanded soft skills, so we held the NEXT conference.The conference was also sponsored by other organizations like PAADC and KwaraBuild. Our resource persons talked on topics like how to overcome depression, how to overcome imposter syndrome, how to excel at public speaking and presentation, how to leverage tech to build wealth and also learning soft skills such as entrepreneurship skills, negotiation skills. Certificates were awarded to participants. ...
